Get going in three steps

  1. Create an account on the Renji site and copy your API key.
  2. Choose how your model connects — directly from this folder (local model), or a remote self-hosted GPU vessel (you give its URL). See the Docs.
  3. Put your key + the heart URL in .env, run ./run.sh, and open the Console.
